Senthil Kumar Durai, MS.

MBBS, MS - Orthopaedics

Dr. Senthil is an orthopaedic consultant and his areas of specialization are hip and knee arthroplasty, complex trauma, and shoulder and knee arthroscopy. With over a decade of experience, Dr Senthil has also trained in trauma, damage control orthopedic surgery, arthroplasty, pelvic-acetabular injuries, paediatric orthopedics, tumour surgery, and spinal injuries and diseases.

Dr Senthil continues to learn new skills and stay abreast of all developments in his speciality, in an endeavour to offer his patients the options of the best and latest treatment options.

education

MBBS, Madras Medical College, Chennai. MS (Orthopaedics), SCB Medical college, Cuttack. Trauma fellow, Guro Hospital, Korea University Medical Center

Experience

Experienced Senior Consultant with a demonstrated history of working in the hospital & health care industry. Skilled in Clinical Research, Medical Education, Medicine, Emergency Medicine, and Hospitals. Strong consulting professional graduated from Korea University.

Member of Foundation
